## **Desperate — annotation guidance**

Adjective: **desperate**  
Category: **Problem**

---

### **1. Value-judgement**  
Almost always yes.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Additionally, there is a **desperate** need for research and interventions in the area of cancer prevention and control for Hispanic populations.*

- *Prostate cancer is in **desperate** need of improved, targeted diagnostic and therapeutic agents.*

</details>

---

### **2. Hyperbolic**  
Yes – inherently hyperbolic.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

NA

</details>

---

### **3a. Gratuitous**  
Often gratuitous.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *There is a **desperate** need for new therapies.* (gratuitous)

- *Patients and physicians are **desperate** for a cure.* (non-gratuitous)

</details>

---

### **3b. Amplified**  
Typically not modified by adverbial boosters.  
May be intensified by (epistemic) stance verbs – i.e. making the claim more certain.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *This demonstrates the **desperate** need for improvement in diagnostic and therapeutic techniques for lung cancer.* (amplified by “demonstrates”)

- *With a median survival time of 14.6 months and approximately 25% of patients alive at 2 years after the most aggressive treatment regimens, there is clearly a **desperate** need to improve the understanding of basic biological processes in this disease.* (amplified by “clearly”)

</details>

---

### **3c. Broader context**  
Possible.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *This work is innovative because we investigate the activity and mechanism of a new drug candidate and new treatment combination for the treatment of PDAC, a cancer in **desperate** need of new therapies.*

</details>

---

### **3d. Grounds**  
Rarely given. May give grounds for a need, but rarely state why it is “desperate”.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Due to the ongoing increase in drug-resistant S. aureus infections, there is a **desperate** need for a successful active vaccine against this ubiquitous pathogen.* (not grounds)

- *This facility is highly used by researchers throughout the region, but still operates with out-of-date instruments that range between 20 to 40 years old, and is thus in **desperate** need of the upgrade described in this proposal.* (grounds)

</details>

---

### **3e. Relative / Hedged**  
Rare.
