### **1. Value-judgement**  
Mixed.

- **Yes** — when used as praise. Typical targets: *research; approach; environment; design; teaching; training; technology; work; intervention; faculty; science*.
- **No** — when used in proper names/labels, or refers neutrally to cognitive processes. Typical targets: *thinking; thought; process(es); expression*.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *It is anticipated that this **creative** and unique research approach will yield information that improves theoretical and real life understanding of the multifaceted and complex issue of withholding / withdrawing life- prolonging treatment .* (value-judgement)

- *All three projects will involve considerable interaction between the investigators on **creative** problem solving .* (value-judgement)

- *Development of **creative** thinking , publication and presentation skills , along with inter-disciplinary team mentoring , and developing and improving faculty mentoring skills , are major features of the program .* (non-value judgement)

</details>

### **3a. Gratuitous**  
Often gratuitous. Possible targets: *use; approach; design; training; solution; program; technology; development; teaching; integration; combination; collaboration; work; intervention; application; plan; infrastructure; effort; discovery; community; cooperation; strategy; method*.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *This project represents an innovative , **creative** method of disseminating historical information and analysis to a broad audience .* (gratuitous)

- *These studies should provide a **creative** approach to prevention and control of prostate cancer that is feasible and cost effective , and should lead to an innovative advance in correlating certain dietary modifications and prostate cancer .* (gratuitous)

- *To immerse the trainees in a **creative** environment and positive research atmosphere which ensures the greatest possibility for success and retention in the ranks of scientific investigators and teachers .* (non-gratuitous)

</details>

---

### **3b. Amplified**  
May be amplified (e.g., *highly*, *especially*, *truly*, *very*, *amazingly*, *exceptionally*).

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Their research is highly **creative** and is supported by five cores that provide innovative services and infrastructure .*

</details>

---

### **3c. Broader context**  
Usually occurs in a promotional context (stacked and/or broader sentential context).

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Our overall goal is to develop the next generation of exceptionally rigorous , **creative** , talented and diverse scientists who are socialized in biotechnology themes and practiced in teamwork .*

</details>
