### **1. Value-judgement**  
Yes — almost always.

<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

</details>

### **3a. Gratuitous**  
Often yes when used in attributive construction.  
Usually no when used as predicate.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *This research will evaluate an **innovative** program of individual and family counseling for caregivers of older people with senile dementia , as a way of reducing the burden of care and making home placement feasible for families who desire it .* (gratuitous)

- *This work is **innovative** in its application of single-channel patch-clamp techniques to a vertebrate neuronal network of known function .* (non-gratuitous)

</details>

---

### **3b. Amplified**  
Often yes. Examples: *highly, most, very, truly, extremely, uniquely, entirely*.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *These highly **innovative** studies will dramatically increase our understanding of the mechanisms by which physical activity improves human health and may provide the basis for future therapeutics to combat many chronic diseases .*

</details>

---

### **3c. Broader context**  
Often stacked with other promotional adjectives.  
Broader sentential context may also contain promotional elements.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *The four projects , representing **innovative** and exciting new initiatives on campus are : 1 ) In Vivo Imaging of Gene Expression in Prostate Cancer , 2 ) Non-Invasive Monitoring of T Cell-Mediated Tumor Ablation , 3 ) Imaging Cancer Viruses with Tat Transducible Peptides , 4 ) Imaging MDR1 P-glycoprotein Transport Activity In Vivo with Tc-94m-Sestamibi PET to Predict Response to Chemotherapy in Extensive Stage Small Cell Lung Cancer .*

</details>

---

### **3d. Grounds**  
Often yes.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *The **innovative** aspect of this study is the selective covalent immobilization of biologically active molecules to titanium .*  
- *The use of simulations to test skills makes our program **innovative** .*  
- *This project is technologically **innovative** because the IPN composition is novel in several respects .*  
- *This proposal thus represents an **innovative** , pragmatic approach to a problem — self-regulatory difficulty after mTBI — that is widespread , under-treated , and for which Veterans desperately want new therapies .*

</details>

---

### **3e. Relative / Hedged**  
Possible, but not common.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *We also develop potentially **innovative** ways to assess circumstances under which cooperative models better match behavior than non-cooperative models .*  
- *The model identifiability analysis for ODE models is also relatively **innovative** from a statistical perspective .*

</details>
