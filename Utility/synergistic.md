## **Synergistic — annotation guidance**

Adjective: **synergistic**  
Category: **UTILITY**

---

### **1. Value-judgement**

Usually YES. *Synergistic* implies that combined elements produce greater value or effectiveness.  

NO when descriptive or mechanistic in biology where synergy is simply an interaction pattern.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *The **synergistic** combination of basic and clinician-scientists places the CEET in a strong position…* (YES)

- *Determine whether the effects are additive or **synergistic**.* (NO)

</details>

---

### **2. Hyperbolic**

No.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

NA

</details>

---

### **3a. Gratuitous**

YES when *synergistic* could be removed without changing the substantive claim (e.g. *team, environment, collaboration, program,* or *interaction*).  

NO when synergy itself is the hypothesis or scientific phenomenon under investigation.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *The TCC will create an inclusive and collaborative virtual environment… into a **synergistic** training effort.* (YES)

- *We propose to test for **synergistic** reactivation of latent HIV in latently infected cells…* (NO)

</details>

---

### **3b. Amplified**

Possible — e.g. *highly.*

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *The Duke Center will promote a highly **synergistic** and interdisciplinary environment…*

</details>

---

### **3c. Broader context**

Frequently YES. *Synergistic* often appears within clusters of positive adjectives such as *collaborative, integrated, multidisciplinary,* and *innovative.*

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *…a coordinated, integrated and **synergistic** environment that facilitates maximal productivity and scientific excellence.* (YES)

- *This proposal truly **synergistic** and unique.* (YES)

</details>

---

### **3d. Grounds**

Often YES. The adjective is frequently supported by an explanation of complementary mechanisms.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *The combination of growth factors will exhibit a **synergistic** effect by improving the self-assembled constructs’ functional properties…* (YES)

- *Using a **synergistic** combination of experimental and computational methods, we will test this model…* (YES)

</details>

---

### **3e. Relative / Hedged**

Frequently YES. Synergy is often framed as hypothetical — e.g. *may, potential, determine whether,* or *suggest.*

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Clinical logic and emerging empirical data suggest that integrating CM with PST may have a **synergistic** effect…* (YES)

- *…we do not know whether intervention in multiple signaling pathways will have a **synergistic** effect…* (YES)

</details>
