## **Impactful — annotation guidance**

Adjective: **impactful**  
Category: **UTILITY**

---

### **1. Value-judgement**

Usually YES — evaluates work, findings, careers, or interventions as having substantial influence or significance. It is a more promotional substitute for *important, influential,* or *practice-changing.*

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *This project represents a unique, **impactful**, and cost-effective opportunity to advance our understanding of hoarding symptoms in ADRD.*

</details>

---

### **2. Hyperbolic**

No.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

NA

</details>

---

### **3a. Gratuitous**

Often YES — removing *impactful* often leaves the proposition unchanged.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *The team has a strong track record of **impactful** research on typical and atypical communication…*

</details>

---

### **3b. Amplified**

Common — often modified by *highly, immensely, particularly,* or *broadly.*

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *This highly **impactful** project will allow us to improve upon delivery of chronic VNS…*

</details>

---

### **3c. Broader context**

Frequently YES — commonly co-occurs with promotional terms such as *innovative, transformative, rigorous, novel, significant,* and *successful.*

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *This project is innovative and **impactful** because it is the first in the field…*

</details>

---

### **3d. Grounds**

Sometimes YES — impact may be justified by downstream outcomes, translational relevance, public health benefit, or mechanistic insight.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Characterizing Kv4 modulation will be broadly **impactful** because ISA critically shapes electrical signaling throughout the nervous system and in the heart.*

</details>

---

### **3e. Relative / Hedged**

Uncommon but possible — modified by *potentially, more, most, particularly,* or *could be.*

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *This study has the potential to be an **impactful** study in the field of HT and BP genetics…*

</details>
