## **Accessible — annotation guidance**

Adjective: **accessible**  
Category: **UTILITY**

---

### **1. Value-judgement**  
YES when it adds a positive usability gloss (*easy to access/use*).

NO when it is literal, technical, or definitional.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

**YES**

- *widely **accessible** therapy*

- *easily **accessible** resources*

- *highly **accessible** intervention*

- *Construct five waves of **accessible** and well-documented panel data.* (YES)

- *Recent studies from our laboratory have isolated a new population of stem cells from human gingiva, an easily **accessible** tissue source, namely GMSC, which exhibit not only multipotent differentiation and self-renewal capacities, but also possess distinct immunomodulatory functions.* (YES)

**NO**

- *anatomically **accessible** regions*

- *not **accessible** to observation*

- ***accessible** samples such as plasma*

- *Without an adequate high-risk paradigm, predispositional variables will not be **accessible** to investigation.* (NO)

</details>

---

### **2. Hyperbolic**  

No.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

NA

</details>

---

### **3a. Gratuitous**  

YES when removable without changing core meaning.

NO when required for meaning.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

**YES**

- ***accessible** database → database*

- ***accessible** intervention → intervention*

- *In addition, the NCGC has worked to advance a number of informatic initiatives to make better use of existing drug and disease target information and provide the general public with easily **accessible** resources, further catalyzing the development of new therapies for human disease.*

**NO**

- *make data **accessible** to all scientists*

- ***accessible** population*

- *not **accessible** to patients*

</details>

---

### **3b. Amplified**  

Possible.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Facilitate research by collecting, storing and distributing a highly **accessible**, but appropriately safeguarded, repository of well-prepared brain tissue and neuropathologic data from carefully and longitudinally characterized patients with mild cognitive impairment (MCI) or dementia as well as noncognitively impaired control individuals using a variety of methods that maximizes utility to scientists.*

</details>

### **3d. Grounds**  

YES when accessibility is functionally justified.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- ***accessible** via any internet-connected device*

- *more **accessible** due to non-invasive sampling*

- ***accessible** because of surface localization*

</details>

---

### **3e. Relative / Hedged**  

YES when comparative or limited.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *more **accessible***

- *relatively **accessible***

- *not **accessible***

</details>
