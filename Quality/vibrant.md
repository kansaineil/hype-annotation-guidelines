### **1. Value-judgement**  

YES — positive evaluation (lively, active, productive, dynamic).

Typical targets: *environment, community, program, network, research area, field.*

Examples:  

*vibrant research environment*  

*vibrant community*  

*vibrant program*

<details markdown="1">

<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *The diverse clinical experience, emphasis on teaching, and **vibrant** neuroscience environment, with special expertise in neuro-infectious diseases, provided by Johns Hopkins Neurology, and Dr. Avindra Nath's laboratory in particular, will provide me with the guidance and opportunities that I need to become a successful, independent clinical researcher.*

</details>
