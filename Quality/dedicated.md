### **1. Value-judgement**  

Usually YES.

YES when used to praise people or groups for commitment.  

Typical targets: *researchers; staff; faculty; team.*

Often also YES when used for resources or spaces designated for a purpose, because it suggests special provision or commitment.  

Typical targets: *facility; unit; clinic; program; resource; space.*

<details markdown="1">

<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Our cancer research programs emphasize translational and interdisciplinary research, and we pursue this goal by promoting collaboration among a diverse and **dedicated** team of outstanding basic, translational, clinical, and population researchers.*

- *This **dedicated** space is a major advancement in the continuing efforts that began in 1997 to establish a formal library.*

</details>
