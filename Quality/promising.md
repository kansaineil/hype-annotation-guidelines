### **1. Value-judgement**  

Usually YES — expresses a positive evaluation (future potential).

<details markdown="1">

<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *One of these 3 trials, designated RV144, has shown modest efficacy in humans and this result coupled with **promising** results from nonhuman primate (NHP) studies has now stimulated the field to continue to test more advanced vaccine candidates in humans.* (YES)

</details>

### **3b. Amplified**  

Possible.

<details markdown="1">

<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *The discovery of the potent and highly selective salicylihalamides, possessing most likely a novel mechanism of action is, thus, a very **promising** starting point for the potential development of a new class of anticancer agents.*

</details>

### **3c. Broader context**  

Often YES.

<details markdown="1">

<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *With a highly competitive residency program, and 6 OB/GYN-related fellowship programs, we have a strong internal pool of potential future candidates from which we can recruit highly qualified and **promising** scholars.*

</details>
