### **1. Value-judgement**  

Mixed.

YES — when used to praise competence or expertise.  

NO — when used descriptively in a technical or comparative sense.

<details markdown="1">

<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *The AccuNurse System has been successfully developed in Phase I to provide an easy-to-use "mentor" and communications tool for CNAs and nurses in **skilled** nursing units.* (YES)

- *Protocol implementation will be assured by our exceptionally **skilled** management team and trainers from UT Southwestern's previous and ongoing multi-site clinical trials and a cadre of CTP sites with significant collaborative research experience.* (YES)

- *It is also expected that there will be developmental differences in how these words are processed with younger and less **skilled** readers relying more heavily on a compositional strategy than older and more skilled readers.* (NO)

- ***Skilled** movements and isometric force production are evaluated in terms of these three control mechanisms.* (NO)

</details>
