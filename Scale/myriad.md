### **1. Value-judgement**
- Almost always. Rhetorical quantifier (“very many”) evaluating number/variety. Can usually be replaced with more neutral words.
- Typical nouns: *diseases/disorders/conditions/symptoms/complications; challenges/issues/barriers/uncertainties; roles/functions/effects/processes/changes; pathways/factors/aspects/ways; studies/research/applications/opportunities/resources.*

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Smoking and tobacco dependence cause **myriad** diseases and almost half a million deaths in the United States each year, including one-third of all cancer deaths.*

</details>
