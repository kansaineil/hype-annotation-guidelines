### **1. Value-judgement**
Yes. Evaluates magnitude or impact as extreme (â‰ˆ very large, huge). Typically modifies abstract or collective nouns where size is impressionistic, e.g.: *potential, impact, burden, implications, value, progress, promise, challenge, benefit, importance, cost, effort, need*. Can usually be removed or replaced (*large, substantial*).

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Probiotics hold **enormous** potential for promoting human health and treating disease, including NEC.*

</details>

### **3d. Grounds**
Possible, but rare. May be justified with evidence.

<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

</details>
