### **1. Value-judgement**
Probable value judgement when used for urgency / priority framing. Possible head nouns: *goal, objective, need, priority, concern, threat, danger, challenge, problem, focus, attention, action, plan, strategy, decision, proposal, solution, assistance, aid, help, access, implementation, requirement, dissemination, translation, applicability, application, relevance, benefit*.

Unlikely value judgement when used for temporal or proximal meaning. Possible head nouns:

- *treatment, therapy, response, effect, onset, release, reaction, diagnosis, recovery, survival, mortality, death, injury, damage, symptom, protection, repair, activation, signaling, phosphorylation, precursor, progenitor, hypersensitivity, clearance, stabilization, transfusion, resuscitation*
- *vicinity, proximity, area, region, environment, microenvironment, surroundings, neighborhood, neighbor, family, relative, descendant*
- *postpartum, posttest, post-intervention, posttreatment, future, term, timeframe, period, aftermath*

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *This application describes the **immediate** needs of our NIH funded research faculty for an instrument capable of both two-photon imaging, providing the Z-depth resolution needed to capture protein movement in living tissue, as well as high-speed resonance scanning with an integrated perfect focus system to capture fast cellular events.*

</details>
