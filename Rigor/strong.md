### **3a. Gratuitous**  
Often gratuitous when modifying abstract or institutional nouns, e.g.:  
*foundation, support, research, track record, commitment, background, record, emphasis, interest, rationale, potential, candidate, history, mentoring, mentorship, training, focus, team, relationship, collaboration, leadership, impact, basis, community, influence, position, tradition, group, role, program, infrastructure, expertise, publication, partnership, desire, preference, implication, case, relevance, motivation, demand, possibility, faculty, impetus, likelihood, indication, grounding, tendency, environment, similarity.*

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *The ARC is built on a **strong** foundation of established and productive research scientists whose purpose is to advance the alcohol research field by promoting excellence in research, training, and education.*

</details>

---

### **3b. Amplified**  
Sometimes amplified (e.g., *very, particularly, exceptionally, extremely, especially, unusually, remarkably, uniquely, exceedingly, so, increasingly, quite, abnormally, extraordinarily, surprisingly, overly, immensely, tremendously*).

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *GSU through its Chemistry Department provides very **strong** support to the Mass Spectrometry Facility with (1) a salary line for a Ph.D.-level Director (PI), (2) a salary line for support at the M.S.-level, (3) instrument service contract support, (4) space support, and (5) business/accounting support.*

</details>

### **3c. Broader context**  
Often stacked with other positive descriptors (e.g., *independent, broad, multidisciplinary, diverse, interdisciplinary, collaborative, productive, interactive, supportive, complementary, experienced, positive, significant, sustainable, durable, reliable, demonstrable, functional, sustained, active, clear, effective, flexible, innovative, resilient*).

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *The UCDHSC BIRCWH Program will play a leading role in the development of a successful cadre of **strong**, independent investigators whose goal through their research is to improve the health of women.*

</details>

### **3e. Relative / Hedged**  
Sometimes framed in relative terms.  
Sometimes hedged (e.g., *relatively, potentially, quite, fairly, likely, moderately, reasonably, apparently, less, not, sufficiently, equally, generally, normally, similarly*).

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Aim 4 is to determine the function of the snoRNA HBII-85 cluster, because there is now relatively **strong** evidence that paternal deficiency for this snoRNA cluster causes the major components of the PWS phenotype.*

</details>
