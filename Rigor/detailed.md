### **1. Value-judgement**  
Usually yes. Typically evaluates depth or thoroughness, not a fixed technical property.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Finally the HRS has **detailed** data on private pension plan provisions .*

</details>

### **3a. Gratuitous**  
Usually gratuitous, especially with abstract or process nouns that denote analysis, knowledge, or description (e.g. *understanding, analysis, information, characterization, knowledge, study, description, mechanism, structure, investigation, examination, data, model, plan, insight, evaluation, assessment, mapping, comparison, measurement, documentation, research, protocol*).

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *This in turn will enable a **detailed** comparison with the natural product , particularly in terms of structure and the specific bioactivity associated with several neurological disease states .*

</details>

---

### **3b. Amplified**  
Sometimes amplified (e.g. *the most, highly, very, increasingly, extremely*).

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *These studies will enable a highly **detailed** understanding of the mechanisms that drive and regulate eukaryotic translation and hold tremendous promise for elucidating the role of translational control in human health and disease .*

</details>

---

### **3c. Broader context**  
Often stacked, especially with other rigour adjectives (e.g. *accurate and detailed, comprehensive and detailed, systematic and detailed, extensive and detailed, thorough and detailed, precise and detailed, robust and detailed, rigorous and detailed*).

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *The goal of the project is to produce a **detailed** and accurate picture of selected inter - and intramolecular distributions for the chosen biomolecules in aqueous solution which will be compared with new experimental structural studies such as those produced by Xray and neutron diffraction difference measurements and NMR / NOE experiments .*

</details>

### **3e. Relative / Hedged**  
Often framed in relative terms (e.g. *more detailed analysis*).  
Usually not hedged.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Thus , a more **detailed** study is required to better define glucose - , and glucose - dependent IGF - 1 / GH-induced Beta-cell growth.*

</details>
