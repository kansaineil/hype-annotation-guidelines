---

### **1. Value-judgement**  
Yes. Expresses strong positive stance.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *The field of stem cell biology is advancing at an **incredible** speed, with new techniques emerging on a regular basis allowing for deeper investigations into the mechanisms underlying adult stem cell biology and tissue regeneration.*

</details>

---

### **2. Hyperbolic**  
Yes (lexically extreme).

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Decision support, surveillance, and quality care initiatives have **incredible** potential to increase the health of veterans.*

</details>

---

### **3a. Gratuitous**  
Almost always. Can usually be deleted with no change to propositional meaning.  

Common head nouns: *ability, accuracy, achievement, adaptation, advance, amount, array, asset, breadth, burden, challenge, complexity, degree, depth, diversity, dynamics, experience, feat, foundation, growth, heterogeneity, importance, improvement, insight, loss, need, number, obstacle, opportunity, population, power, progress, promise, range, rate, specificity, speed, throughput, utility, wealth*

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Recent years have seen **incredible** growth in methods for localizing neural sources, and currently available techniques offer strong possibilities for inferring ACC and PFC sources using ERP, magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), and combined (multimodal) ERP and MRI approaches.*

</details>
