### **2. Hyperbolic**  
Yes.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *These studies offer the **exciting** prospect of yielding high resolution structural information of a lipid-requiring membrane enzyme .*

</details>

### **3a. Gratuitous**  
Almost always. Can usually be deleted without changing propositional meaning.  

Common head nouns: *finding(s), result(s), data, discovery, opportunity, possibility, potential, research, approach, progress, advance, development, field, project*.

Possible exceptions when used as a predicate.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *This builds on **exciting** recent work that suggests that some of the key determinants of smoking progression include community contexts.* (gratuitous)

- *This is **exciting** because a small peptide provides higher potency and targeting than a small molecule while maintaining a smaller size and decreasing toxicity and accumulation potential than the whole protein, and so can be used repeatedly in the same individual.* (non-gratuitous)

</details>

---

### **3b. Amplified**  
Sometimes amplified (e.g. *most, particularly, extremely, very, truly*).

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Based on these very **exciting** findings, we propose to tease out the molecular mechanism underlying forced terminal differentiation of human glioma cells that is mediated by a synergistic action of two transcription factors.*

</details>

---

### **3c. Broader context**  
Often occurs in a promotional context.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Identifying the mechanisms by which p53 regulates SK1 will not only shed light on this **exciting** novel connection between the two components, but will also result in the identification of novel therapeutic targets.*

</details>

---

### **3d. Grounds**  
Possible.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *This emphasis on education and the opportunities for trainees who participate in this meeting makes it an **exciting** training modality.*

</details>

---

### **3e. Relative / Hedged**  
Possible.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *These results suggest that trkBT1 may be an **exciting** new molecular target.*

</details>
