### **3a. Gratuitous**  
Attributive use almost always gratuitous.  

Common head nouns: *aspect, case, candidate, class, compound, data, difference, effect, example, expression, feature, finding, gene, group, hit, hypothesis, implication, information, insight, issue, lead, model, molecule, mutant, mutation, observation, parallel, pattern, phenotype, possibility, problem, property, protein, question, region, result, set, target*

Predicative use (*X is interesting*) is often non-deletable.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *To date, we have had a number of **interesting** findings based on very simple questionnaire outcomes.*

</details>
