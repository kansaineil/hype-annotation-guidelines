### **3a. Gratuitous**  
Often yes when used in attributive construction.  
Common head nouns include: *ability, progress, advance, success, capacity, degree, efficacy, diversity, similarity, growth, feature, change, opportunity, potential, impact, result, record, improvement, observation, phenomenon.*

Ask whether it can be deleted without changing the proposition.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Because of the **remarkable** success of this program and continuing requests for participation by well-qualified young clinicians, we are requesting support for 10 positions.*

</details>

---

### **3b. Amplified**  
Possible – e.g. *truly*.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *This is a truly **remarkable** benefit utilizing a biologic therapy.*

</details>

### **3c. Broader context**
